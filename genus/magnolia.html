Magnolia

  • Since Magnolia emarginata, the Northern Haiti Magnolia, was first discovered in 1925, it went missing for 97 years and the species was considered to be 'possibly extinct'. However, a conservation team re-discovered the species in 2022 in Haiti, where today it is believed that only 1% of original forests remain.
