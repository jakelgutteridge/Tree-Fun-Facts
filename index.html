This website is dedicated to all trees around the world and how they are amazing creations of nature. We aim to promote the importance of trees to Earth and provide an insight into their impact on human culture, significance in history, and anything else that is interesting.

These are things that we must understand in order to appreciate the true value and wonder of trees. They are not only the oldest living organisms in the world, but are an indispensable life source — as such, trees should be highly regarded and respected!

This site is currently in its early stages and is being built upon regularly in order to create a comprehensive resource.
